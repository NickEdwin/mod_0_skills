Class: Menu

Attributes:
appetizers (hash)
items (boolean)
pages (integer)
color (string)

Methods:
appetizerList (modifies the "appetizers" hash with new items (key) and prices (value))
itemsAvailable (modifies the "items" status to true or false for availability)
numberOfPages (modifies the number of "pages" based on menu size)
colorChange (modifies the menu's color to fit needs)
