Class: Menu

Attributes:
appetizerList (hash)
isItemAvailable (boolean)
numberOfPages (integer)
defaultMenuColor (string)

Methods:
modifyAppetizerList (modifies the "appetizerList" hash to add/remove items (key) and prices (value))
changeItemAvailability (manipulates the "isItemAvailable" status to "true" or "false" for availability)
adjustNumberOfPages (modifies "numberOfPages" larger or smaller based on menu size)
changeMenuColor (modifies the "defaultMenuColor" to fit needs)
