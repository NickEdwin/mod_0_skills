Class: Playlist

Attributes:
playlistName (string)
isRadioOn (boolean)
trackList (hash)
currentVolume (float)

Methods:
setPlaylistName (changes "playlistName")
changePowerStatus (changes "isRadioOn" boolean to true or false for on/off)
modifyPlaylist (changes the "trackList" hash to add or remove songs)
adjustVolume (increases or decreases "currentVolume" level)
